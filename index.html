Ritabrata Bose

I’m Ritabrata Bose, currently working as a Research Associate at Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore (IIMB), under Prof. Kunal Dasgupta.

I have completed research master's (M.Phil. Economics) from Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research (IGIDR). I have received my master's degree (M.Sc. Economics) and bachelor's degree (B.Sc. Economics (honours)) from University of Calcutta.

Research Interests: International Trade, Labour Economics and Geography.
