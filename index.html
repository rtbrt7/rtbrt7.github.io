Xuan (Sarah) Zou

Xuan (Sarah) Zou is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Rutgers University. She received her master degree from University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign and bachelor degree from Wuhan University.

Her current research focuses on the slow recovery of small business lending by the U.S. banks after the recent financial crisis. She works with big data from Federal Reserve System to identify the possible credit gap for small businesses and uses various empirical methods to analyze the causes and influences.
